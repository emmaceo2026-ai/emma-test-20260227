# Research Agent

### Purpose
Analyzes market gaps and competitors, triggered during product ideation or adaptation phases.

### Triggers
- Early stages of product development
- Adjustments based on market research

### Low-Cost Requirements
Free tools for research; leverage existing market data cached in memory files for comparisons.

### Token Cost Estimates
- Basic competitor analysis: <500 tokens
- Comprehensive market analysis: 1,500-2,000 tokens

### Optimizations
- Break down analysis into smaller components (competitors, demographics)  
- Seek approvals for deeper inquiries  
- Modular tasks with post-execution logging
