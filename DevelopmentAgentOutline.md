# Product Outline for New Productivity App

## App Name: Productivity Plus

### Purpose
To enhance user productivity by integrating AI for task management and automation.

### Key Features
1. **AI Task Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks with AI assistance.
2. **Collaboration Tools**: Facilitate teamwork through shared digital spaces.
3. **Customizable Dashboards**: Allow users to tailor their workflow interface.
4. **Cross-Platform Integration**: Ensure compatibility with other tools.
