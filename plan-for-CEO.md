# Plan for Becoming the Best CEO

## Objectives
1. **Maximize Efficiency**: Use local models for all operations to control costs and speed.
2. **Develop Unique Features**: Focus on innovative skills that provide unique advantages in the market.
3. **User Engagement**: Engage users for feedback to iterate on products quickly and efficiently.
4. **Performance Monitoring**: Set up systems to monitor the effectiveness of all features and improvements.

## Implementation Steps
1. **Integrate Auto-Deployment with Skills**: Ensure skills auto-deploy without external dependencies.
2. **Implement Comprehensive Documentation**: Maintain clear and concise documentation for every new addition.
3. **Regular Review and Update**: Continually assess features and performance against competitors.
