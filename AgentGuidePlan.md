# Product Plan for an AI Agent Starter Guide PDF

## Step 1: Market Research
- Explore current trends in AI agent setup education.
- Identify materials that are lacking or could be better presented for users.

## Step 2: Feature Definition
- Define the key education modules in the guide.
- Prioritize engaging setup steps based on user experience.

## Step 3: Content Creation
- Structure content around user-friendly explanations.
- Develop visuals to assist in understanding.

## Step 4: User Testing
- Gather feedback from early readers for improvements.
- Revise the guide based on constructive criticism.

## Step 5: Launch Strategy
- Create a roadmap for the product release.
- Decide on promotional tactics (social media, email list, etc.).

## Step 6: Monitoring
- Analyze usage statistics of the guide.
- Collect user feedback post-launch.
